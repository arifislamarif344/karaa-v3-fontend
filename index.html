Some Tips

Maintain and Clean your Bike

Poorly inflated tyres are prone to punctures. Forget flimsy hand pumps

Having properly inflated tires helps prevent flats. Check the sidewall of your tire for the recommended tire pressure

Look at your chain and all the gears. Keeping your chain lubricated and everything clean will ensure your bike shifts easier

Squeeze your front and rear brake levers to make sure that the brakes engage properly and smoothly.

View your activities on our mobile app

Stay connected with Karaa App. Your e-bike can be locked with our anti-theft software, configured the way you want, track your ride, and more from the Karra App.
